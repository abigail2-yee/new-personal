A rotating subset of some of my favourite Sydney food institutions

  • Khanom House's matcha and thai-influenced desserts
  • A.P. Place's Porchetta or Chicken Salad sandwich in bianca dough
  • Brighter Coffee's roasted zucchini and house pickle on sourdough
  • Abdul's Restaurant for a hearty Middle Eastern feast
  • ellu cafe's exquisite french toast
  • Tenacious Bakehouse's Black Garlic Crown (although i haven't seen it served in a while...)
  • Golden Age Cinema & Bar for the sichuan cocktail popcorn (trust...)
  • Golden Lotus Vegan's vegan chicken pho
  • Ora Restaurant for the best Japanese in Sydney deemed by Amanda K :')
  • 胖姨儿手工拉面馆 My Aunt's Handmade Noodles for northern chinese noodles (with free refills!)
  • The Eight for all yumcha needs
